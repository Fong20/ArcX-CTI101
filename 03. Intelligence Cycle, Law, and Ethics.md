## Intelligence Cycle
- The intelligence cycle is at the core of cyber threat intelligence because it provides a structured framework for collecting, processing, analysing, and disseminating information about potential cyber threats.
- There are various specializations within each intelligence cycle process.
- Eg: certain department focuses on collection phase, while another department focuses on analysis phase

### Intelligence Cycle process
There are four process in the intelligence cycle

1. Direction = process of gathering requirements / obtain direction from the customer. This is also known as intelligence requirements.
2. Collection = process of collecting data and converting it into information
3. Analysis = process of transforming the information into intelligence
4. Dissemination = process of handling back the intelligence to the customer, which stimulates a new direction

### Sterile Corridor in intelligence cycle
- Sterile corridor is defined as a deliberate separation between phases of the inteliigence cycle, with the objective of obscuring certain aspect of an intelligence activity to other intelligence team members.
- Eg: Hiding the information of the informant who provided the data from the analyst.

## Law and Ethics in CTI

### Law
- set of rules nad regulations not to break
- usually defined by the goverment or state

### Ethics
- set of guidelines that a person should follow
- usually defined by organization, personal
- Though there may be no legal consequences for choosing not to follow them, ignoring them could result in the loss of employment.

Ambulance chasing = a sales strategy based on following up on a person or organizations's misfortune. It is not illegal but it is classified as unethical
Responsible disclosure = willing disclosure of potential issues
